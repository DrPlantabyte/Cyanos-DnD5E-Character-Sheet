- Regular text field for class, number spinner for total character level, proficiency bonus calculated from total character level
- Ability scores: Icon, Name, Score, -> [Mod], Save Prof.?, <roll check>, <roll prof. check>
- Skills: Icon, Name, Prof. bonus (number spinner), [skill bonus], <roll check>
	- All normal skills plus 2 fill-in-the-blank lines
- Attack: Icon, Weapon/Spell name, Attack Bonus (number spinner), <roll attack>, On hit:, damage, properties/effects, <roll damage>
- Spell Level: Icon, Level #, spells cast (number spinner), / , spells per day (number spinner)
- Spell: prep'd checkbox, Spell, pg. #, components (☑V ☑S ☑M:____), Casting Time, Target & Range, Duration, ☑conc.?, Notes:___
- Pet/Animal Companion/Familiar: mini NPC stat block
